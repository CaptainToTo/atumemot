My First Software Dev Job Went Amazing

The tutoring service I normally work for during the school year has to schedule all of the tutors' sessions at the start of each quarter. This is a massive task that requires the entire staff to crunch for 2 weeks straight. Seeing an opportunity, I approached the department director, and offered to create a tool to help automate the scheduling process over the Summer.
