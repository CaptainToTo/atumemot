Linked Privacy Policy - 7/11/2024

ATUMEMOT does not collect person information on users through Linked. Data is collected by the 3rd-party Unity Ads service for advertisement delivery.


The data collected is:

  • Approximate location
  • Ad interactions and impressions
  • Purchase history
  • Device identifiers

ATUMEMOT does not manage or have access to this data, except for congregate location by country, and impressions statistics.
